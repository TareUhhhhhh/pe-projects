Remember It

Why?

I found myself often going to restaurants and trying to remember if I liked the specific entree that I ordered there. Menus are only so helpful and most descriptions are vague and non-descript.

At times I even find myself struggling to remember the specific brands of items at the grocery store that I prefer! There are two cans of refried beans that are preferable, but I can never remember which one is the absolute best!
