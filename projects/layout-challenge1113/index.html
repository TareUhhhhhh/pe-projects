Cats are wonderful little creatures that bless our presence. Their personalities are all as unique and interesting as any of our human friends!

Laid Back and Lazy

Sometimes cats are super laid back and just lie around the house all day. Many you will find near a window or door letting in some sunshine and warmth. Just be wary of their fluffy little belly.

Wisdom

Grumpy Cats

The late, beloved, Grumpy Cat is the face of how many see all feline friends. Mad at the world and full of hiss and vinegar. Contrary to that face, Tardar Sauce (her actual name) was a sweet and friendly little ball of fluff.

Cranky

Playful Kitties!

Who doesn't love to watch a little furball bat a little jingling ball across the floor?? They exhibit such a carefree playfulness right before stretching out into a catnap.
